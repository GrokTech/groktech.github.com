Applications > Emergency SOS

Emergency SOS app can be used to notify your loved ones of an emergency for their immediate attention.

  • Reaches upto five of your pre-configured contacts with a default text message
  • Initiates a emergency call to a preconfigured number for seeking help
  • Shares your Geo location so that you can be easily located

A highly recommended app for the aged, children, women, people with medical problems and anyone who is in need.
