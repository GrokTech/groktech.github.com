Course Overview

This course is designed for those looking to attain Android application development skills.

Duration: 2 Months for weekday batch
5 Weeks for weekend batch
Batches: Weekday morning batch: 7 AM to 9 AM, Monday – Friday
Weekday evening batch: 7 PM to 9 PM, Monday – Friday
Weekend batch: 9 AM to 6 PM, Saturday & Sunday
