Applications > Backup My Phone

Backup My Phone helps in backing up of data on your phone.

  • Important data on the phone can be backed up to SD card and restored back to phone
  • Contacts, Calendar, Settings, Bookmarks, Application and Application data, gallery content, documents etc. get backed up based on user configuration
  • Option to schedule periodic backups
